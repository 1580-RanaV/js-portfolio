EDUCATION

Embarking on my architectural odyssey, I delved into the realms of creativity and innovation at the prestigious National Institute of Technology, Trichy,from 2015 to 2020. This immersive journey not only sculpted my design acumen but also sculpted a profound comprehension of architectural tenets, sustainable design principles, and the integration of technology in modern architecture. Through collaborative projects and hands-on experiences, I gained insights into urban planning, historic preservation, and the symbiotic relationship between architecture and the environment.

Before crafting architectural marvels, I fortified my academic arsenal at Narayana Institutions in Visakhapatnam,from 2013 to 2015. Here, I sketched the blueprint of my intellect, blending science and mathematics into a harmonious symphony of knowledge. This period of academic rigor not only sharpened my analytica skills but also fostered a deep appreciation for the interdisciplinary nature of architecture, where science, art, and engineering converge to create impactful spaces.

My artistic voyage commenced at Delhi Public School in Visakhapatnam,where in 2013, the seeds of curiosity and creativity were sown. These early strokes on the canvas of education ignited a passion that continues to shape my vision as an architect. The exposure to diverse cultures, languages, and perspectives during my formative years laid the foundation for a holistic approach to design, rooted in empathy and cultural sensitivity.
